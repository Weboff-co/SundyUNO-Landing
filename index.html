Introducing Sundy UNO: The Ultimate Hardware Encryption Device

At Sundy, we understand that in today's digital age, the security of your sensitive data is paramount. That's why we've developed Sundy UNO, a groundbreaking hardware encryption device designed to offer unparalleled protection and convenience.

Unmatched Security: Our device uses AES256 encryption and unlimited password-protected crypto containers to keep your data secure.

Transparency in Action:We believe in proving our claims, and this challenge is our way of showing you the reliability of Sundy UNO.

Customer Involvement:Engage with our brand in a unique, interactive way that demonstrates our commitment to your data protection.

Here’s how it works:

Preinstalled Crypto Containers

Every Sundy UNO device comes with 120 preinstalled crypto containers. Each container securely stores a mnemonic phrase for a dedicated Bitcoin (BTC) wallet.

Initial Deposit

We’ve placed 1 BTC in the first wallet.

Monthly Transfers: 

At the end of every month for the next 10 years, 1 BTC will be moved to the next BTC wallet.

Password Release

At the end of each month, we will publicly release the password for the previous crypto container.
